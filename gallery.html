Essence Room

The Essence Room, our intimate event space. With seating for up to 14 guests, this cozy wooden sanctuary is perfect for private meetings. Enjoy a separate welcome bar area featuring an exquisite whiskey and cognac selection.

Grandeur Hall

Welcome to the Grandeur Hall, where timeless elegance meets contemporary design. Accommodating up to 180 guests, and with opulent architecture, rich upholstery tones, and impactful lighting, this exquisite event space sets the stage for unforgettable gatherings.

Radiance Hall

Introducing the Radiance Hall, our exquisite event space nestled on the upper floor. With seating for up to 40 guests, this airy and well-lit room offers breathtaking views of the main dining area.
